I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the Research School of Economics, Australian National University (ANU). My primary research interests are Game Theory and Decision Theory. 

Currently, my research focuses on:
* **The Economics of Cybersecurity:** Developing game-theoretic models to understand cyber defense strategies, attacker behavior, and the pricing of cybersecurity tools.
* **Superstition and Divination:** Exploring how concepts like cheap talk, confirmatory bias, and Bayesian updating can model superstitious behaviors and decision-making.
* **Platform Economics & Two-Sided Markets:** Analyzing the microeconomic literature concerning accessory goods and digital platforms.

Please feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:yuechen.wang@anu.edu.au) if you'd like to discuss research, game theory, or potential collaborations!
